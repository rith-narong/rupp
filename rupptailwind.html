About RUPP

The Royal University of Phnom Penh (RUPP) is Cambodia’s oldest and largest university. It hosts more than 12000 scholarship and full-fee paying students, across a diverse range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs. It is unique in Cambodia for offering specialist degrees in fields including the sciences, humanities and social sciences, as well as vocational courses in fields such as information technology, electronics, psychology and tourism. RUPP also provides Cambodia’s foremost degree-level language programs through the Institute of Foreign Languages. Due to its many achievements, RUPP is the first university in Cambodia with full membership of the ASEAN University Network (AUN).

History

The Royal University of Phnom Penh first opened its doors as the Royal Khmer University on January 13, 1960, with a National Institute of Judicial and Economic Studies, a Royal School of Medicine, a National School of Commerce, a National Pedagogical Institute, a Faculty of Letters and Human Sciences.

Mission

The three-fold mission of the Program is to Provide high-quality teaching and learning opportunities to prepare professionals for leadership in a wide variety of educational careers; Engage in scholarly research and prepare scholars who can conduct academic research that addresses critical national and regional educational issues; and Provide valuable service to the field through collaboration with other educational agencies, institutions, and scholars to contribute to the improvement of the education system in Cambodia and the region.

Vision

The Program will be the leading institute in Cambodia committed to the teaching and learning of current knowledge and the development of new knowledge and skills for current and future educators and educational leaders. This will be done through the provision of high quality academic education programs of study, engagement in research and involvement in service. These activities will contribute to the ongoing development of a theoretically informed and more equitable educational system in the country and in the broader region.
